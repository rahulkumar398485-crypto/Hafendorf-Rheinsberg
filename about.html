Our Story

Sunday Resort Hafendorf Rheinsberg

A Resort by the Water

Welcome to Sunday Resort Hafendorf Rheinsberg – your retreat by the water, where relaxation, nature, and stylish comfort come together. Located in the picturesque Mecklenburg Lake District, the resort offers a unique combination of tranquility and maritime atmosphere.

Perfect for Every Stay

Whether you are looking for a relaxing holiday, an active weekend or a special break, you will find it here. Enjoy spacious rooms and suites, culinary highlights, wellness experiences and explore the surrounding lake landscape, historic Rheinsberg and its famous castle.

Location

Lake Rheinsberg

Located in the Mecklenburg Lake District, the resort is surrounded by nature, water and scenic landscapes ideal for relaxation and outdoor activities.

Nearby Attractions:

  • Rheinsberg Castle
  • Ruppiner Seenland
  • Stechlin Running Park
  • Lake District cycling routes

Why Guests Love It

  • Direct lake access and marina
  • Perfect for boating and water sports
  • Beautiful natural surroundings
  • Ideal for hiking and cycling
What We Offer

Experience the Best of Rheinsberg

Comfortable Rooms & Suites

Maritime-style rooms and elegant suites with views of the harbor, lighthouse and surrounding countryside.

Wellness & Spa

2,000 m² wellness area with pool, sauna, treatments and relaxation spaces.

Gastronomy

Regional and international cuisine in restaurants and bars with a beautiful lake view.

Marina Experience

Discover the marina, rent boats or enjoy life directly on the water.
