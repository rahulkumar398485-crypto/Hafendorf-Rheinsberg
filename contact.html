Getting Here

Travel to Lake Rheinsberg

Travel via Berlin to Rheinsberg station. From there, the resort is just a short taxi ride away.

Easily accessible from Berlin (approx. 1.5 hours). Follow directions towards Rheinsberg in Brandenburg.

Nearest airport: Berlin Brandenburg Airport (BER), approx. 100 km from the resort.
