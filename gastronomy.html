A Culinary Journey

The Sunday Resort Hafendorf Rheinsberg offers a wide range of culinary experiences. Restaurants and bars are the central meeting place for breakfast, dinner or a relaxing drink by the water. Guests enjoy creative regional and international specialties in a stylish maritime atmosphere.

LUV & LEE Restaurant

The half-board restaurant offers fresh regional cuisine right on the marina. Enjoy fish, meat and vegetarian dishes with a beautiful view of Lake Rheinsberg.

Breakfast: 7:00 – 10:30

Dinner: 17:30 – 20:00 (Fri–Sat until 21:00)

Seaport Bar & Restaurant

A relaxed dining experience with regional and international dishes in a modern maritime atmosphere directly at the harbor.

Open seasonally

Harbor Bar

Enjoy cocktails, wines and refreshing drinks with a stunning waterfront view. The perfect place to relax after a day at the lake.

Open daily

Nightclub 53°/12°

Experience vibrant nightlife with music, cocktails and a stylish atmosphere directly by the waterfront.

Open on select days
