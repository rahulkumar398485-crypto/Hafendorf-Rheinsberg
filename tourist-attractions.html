Lake District Adventures

Experience the Beauty of Rheinsberg

The Sunday Resort Hafendorf Rheinsberg is located in the beautiful Mecklenburg Lake District. Surrounded by lakes, forests and historic towns, the region offers endless opportunities for relaxation, exploration and outdoor activities.

Top Attractions

Discover the Region

Ruppiner Seenland

A beautiful lake region connecting Berlin with the Mecklenburg Lake District, perfect for boating, canoeing and exploring nature.

Stechlin Running Park

Over 350 km of marked trails for running, hiking and Nordic walking in a stunning natural environment.

Cycling Tours

Discover the “land of a thousand lakes” by bike with scenic routes through forests, villages and along waterways.

Hiking Trails

A wide network of hiking trails offers year-round experiences in nature with lakes, forests and wildlife.

Rheinsberg Castle

A historic cultural highlight where Frederick the Great once lived, surrounded by beautiful gardens and lakes.
